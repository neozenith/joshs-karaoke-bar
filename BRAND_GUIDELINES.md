# Josh's Karaoke Bar - Brand Guidelines

## Brand Overview

Josh's Karaoke Bar is an authentic Japanese-style karaoke venue in the heart of Melbourne CBD, capturing the electric energy of Tokyo's karaoke culture with the grungy, artistic spirit of Melbourne's famous laneways.

**Location:** 13 Swift Lane, Melbourne 3000
**Hours:** 4pm - 2am Daily
**Vibe:** Urban grunge meets authentic Japanese nightlife

**Tagline:** "At Josh's Karaoke Bar, Everyone is your friend you haven't met yet."

---

## Brand Personality

**Adjectives:** Energetic, Authentic, Urban, Fun, Inclusive, Vibrant, Grungy-Cool

- **Electric & Alive:** Like the neon-lit streets of Tokyo and Melbourne's graffiti laneways
- **Authentic:** Real Japanese karaoke experience with Japanese street food
- **Inclusive:** From birthday parties to corporate events, everyone's welcome
- **Urban Edge:** Melbourne's laneway culture with street art aesthetic

## Typography

### Primary Font: Bebas Neue
- **Usage:** Main headings, hero text, section titles, CTAs
- **Why:** Bold, condensed, and impactful. Channels street poster and graffiti aesthetics while maintaining readability. Perfect for the urban karaoke vibe.
- **Google Font:** `Bebas Neue`

### Secondary Font: Inter
- **Usage:** Body text, navigation, UI elements, descriptions
- **Why:** Modern, highly readable, and versatile. Provides excellent contrast to Bebas Neue while maintaining a contemporary feel.
- **Google Font:** `Inter` (weights: 400, 500, 600, 700)

### Hierarchy Example
```
H1: Bebas Neue, 72px (mobile: 48px)
H2: Bebas Neue, 56px (mobile: 36px)
H3: Bebas Neue, 40px (mobile: 28px)
Body: Inter Regular, 16px
Button: Bebas Neue, 18px
```

## Voice & Tone

### Voice Characteristics
- **Energetic but not shouty:** "Get ready to sing your heart out!"
- **Welcoming & inclusive:** "Whether it's your first time or you're a karaoke pro..."
- **Authentic & knowledgeable:** "Authentic Japanese street food" not "Asian fusion"

### Example Copy
✅ **Do:** "Book your private karaoke room and unleash your inner rockstar"

✅ **Do:** "Melbourne's authentic Japanese karaoke experience"

## Key Messaging

### Official Tagline
**"At Josh's Karaoke Bar, Everyone is your friend you haven't met yet."**

This tagline emphasizes the welcoming, inclusive, and community-focused nature of the venue. It captures the social magic of karaoke and positions Josh's as a place where strangers become friends through shared musical experiences.

### Alternative Messaging

- "Sing Your Heart Out in Melbourne's Laneways"
- "Where Tokyo Meets Melbourne"

1. **Authentic Japanese Experience:** State-of-the-art equipment, Japanese street food
2. **Versatile Venue:** 8 private rooms + main bar area
3. **Perfect for Any Occasion:** Birthdays, corporate, parties, casual nights
4. **Melbourne Location:** Heart of CBD, classic laneway venue
5. **Quality:** Premium sound systems, extensive song library, great food & drinks

## Usage Guidelines

### DO
- Use neon colors strategically for energy and emphasis
- Maintain high contrast for readability (light text on dark backgrounds)
- Keep layouts clean despite the vibrant aesthetic
- Use Bebas Neue for impact, Inter for clarity
- Include photography that shows people having fun

### DON'T
- Overuse neon colors (they should pop, not overwhelm)
- Use generic stock photography
- Set body text in Bebas Neue (readability issues)
- Forget white space - even grungy designs need breathing room
- Make it too busy - focus on key messages
